01. Our Goal

Our Goal

Hello Students!

In this "mini" Linear Algebra lesson we will cover very important tools in Linear Algebra. You will need these tools as a mathematical foundation for understanding and creating a Neural Network.

This lesson is NOT a complete stand-alone Linear Algebra class.

Our goal is to give you the fundamental tools you will need to train neural networks while showing you the beautiful visual world of Vectors and Matrices.
