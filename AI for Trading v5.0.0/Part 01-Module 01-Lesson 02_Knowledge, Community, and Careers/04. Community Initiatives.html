04. Community Initiatives

Welcome to the community!

You’re now part of a global network of lifelong learners just like yourself! We often hear from our alumni how vital this community was to their success, and we can’t wait to see what lies in store for you!

Meet your Classmates

You already saw the "Communities" part of the Student Hub under the Peer Collaboration section in the previous concept. Now go ahead and introduce yourself to your fellow classmates. You can share things like "Where you are from?" , "What are you currently doing?" , "Why are you here?" etc to start a conversation.

Make Each Project a Milestone

Break your Nanodegree program into smaller milestones with projects. Take part in our Level Up Challenge and get digitally recognized for each project you complete. Every step counts towards your goal of graduation!

Commit to Learning Every Day

Challenge yourself to do 30 Days of Udacity and visit your Classroom every day. By devoting at least 30 minutes a day to your studies, you’ll be building a habit that leads to success. See your peers taking part in the challenge in Student Hub here .

Find out more information about all of these Initiatives on our Community Site here .
